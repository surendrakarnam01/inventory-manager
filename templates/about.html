What is Inventory Manager?

Inventory Manager is a simple web app that lets each user maintain their own product list. Add products, edit details, track quantities, and quickly spot low stock items with a warning badge when quantity is 1 or less.

🧠

Designed for clarity

A clean dashboard shows totals and categories at a glance, plus an easy-to-use products table.

👤

Private per user

Products are stored with a `user_id`, so every logged-in user sees only their own inventory.

⚙️

Simple CRUD flow

Add, edit, and delete products with protected routes and instant updates on the dashboard.
